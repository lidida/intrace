Our Solution

INTRACE - the next-generation system for traceability in the textile industry. The solution enables all stakeholders across the supply chain to access up-to-date information about the lifecycle of a garment and its environmental impact, thereby helping them make data-driven decisions and close the production loop.

INTRACE Intel

A digital platform for suppliers that acts as a centralized repository of all order-related and subcontractor-related information (production steps, supplier profiles, rankings, audits, and certifications) as well as provides visualized analytics of the garments’ sustainability metrics.

INTRACE Tag

A physical tag embedded into the garment with dual interface: end-customers can access sustainability data about their garment and recycling companies can access fiber/ chemical composition data to help automate the sorting process and provide wear analytics back to the platform.

INTRACE App

An app that allows end-customers to access sustainability data about their garment once it is scanned via a smartphone. The app provides information about the garment’s ecological footprint as well as the recycling guidelines and locations.

INTRACE Intel: How It Works

1Order Module
Make data-driven decisions when choosing suppliers and materials based on garment’s environmental footprint.
2Supplier Module
Use insights gained about suppliers’ certification record & environmental performance for continuous improvement.
3Trace Module
Access new data: garments' production history, sustainability & environmental footprint, wear length and waste recuperation.
